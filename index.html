‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is FORGE? ‚¨á
FORGE is a comprehensive framework and automation system designed to streamline Burning Man camp planning and execution. It integrates preparation, building, participation, and cleanup phases with the 10 Principles at its core.
How do I join the community? ‚¨á
Simply click the "Join Our Discord" button at the top of the page. Discord is where we collaborate, share updates, and build relationships with fellow campers and volunteers.
What do I need to bring to Burning Man? ‚¨á
Check out our "What to Bring" guide in the Survival Guide section. It covers essentials like water, sun protection, camping gear, and other items necessary for a safe and comfortable playa experience.
Can I volunteer? ‚¨á
Absolutely! We have many volunteer roles available. Check the "Volunteering Opportunities" section to learn about different ways you can contribute to making our camp experience amazing.
What are the 10 Principles? ‚¨á
The 10 Principles are the philosophical foundation of Burning Man: Radical Inclusion, Gifting, Decommodification, Radical Self-Reliance, Radical Self-Expression, Communal Effort, Civic Responsibility, Leave No Trace, Participation, and Immediacy. Everything we do honors these values.
How is FORGE organized? ‚¨á
FORGE operates in four phases: Preparation (planning and organizing), Build (construction and setup), The Burn (the event itself), and Clean Up (restoration and reflection). Each phase has dedicated tools and resources in our workflow system.
